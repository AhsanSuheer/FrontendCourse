Golden retrievers are very cute

Let's investigate why

Is it the fur?

Very furry boy for example

Reports are coming in that people absolutely love these furry boys. We think that this may be an important part of why they are so cute, however we need more data.

Is it their energy?

People have forever been complaining that these good boys are just too energetic. They jump and jump and roll until they're almost tired, but then they roll some more.

Is it their tongues?

People have also been complaining that these floofs are completely incapable of controlling their tongues. They just really can't stop being happy, ever.
